Law Enforcement Requests

Updated over a week ago

Anthropic PBC discloses account records solely in accordance with our Terms of Service and applicable law. For more information about Anthropic’s policies for handling governmental requests for user information, see here. If you are a law enforcement agent who is authorized to gather evidence in connection with an official investigation, you may request records from Anthropic by emailing [email protected].

Please include the following information with your request:

  • Law Enforcement Agency Country

  • City

  • State/Province

  • Zip/Postal Code

  • Law Enforcement Address

  • Name of Court/Policy Department/Authority/Agency

  • Name of Contact Person Handling this Matter (Detective, Investigator, etc.)

  • Official Law Enforcement Agency Request Email Address

  • Contact Person’s Phone Number

  • Description of legal Inquiry

  • Matter Type of legal Inquiry

  • Attach Any Relevant Documentation (e.g., Subpoena, Warrant)

  • NCMEC Report Identifier (If Applicable)
