‍What are Cookies?

‍

Like every other professional website, this website uses cookies. Cookies are tiny files that are downloaded to your computer, to improve your experience. This page will tell you what information we collect, how we use it, and why we sometimes need to store these cookies. We will also tell you how you can prevent these cookies from being stored, however, this may downgrade or 'break' certain elements of the website’s functionality.

‍

How do we use Cookies?

‍

We set the following cookies to provide you with an optimum website experience:

‍

A) Site preferences cookies

‍

To provide you with a great website experience, we let you decide how this website runs for you by asking for your setting preferences. To remember your preferences, we set cookies so that this information is recalled every time you interact with a page on this website.

‍

B) Third-party Cookies

‍

In some special cases, we also use cookies provided by trusted third parties. 

‍

Here are some third-party cookies that you may encounter through this website.

‍

  • Third-party analytics are used to track and measure the usage of this website so that we can continue to produce engaging content for you. 
  • These third-party cookies may track things such as - how much time you spend on this website, the pages you visit on this website, etc. - helping us to understand how we can improve the website for you.

‍

How to disable Cookies?

‍

Unfortunately, in most cases, there are no industry standard options for disabling cookies without completely disabling the functionality and features they add to the website. If you are unsure what to do, it is recommended that you leave all cookies on as they may be used to provide a service that you need.

‍

You can disable cookies by adjusting your browser settings. Be aware that disabling cookies will affect the functionality of all the websites that you visit including Rephrase.ai. Therefore it is not recommended to disable cookies.
